Dr. Mohsin Reza

Dr. Mohsin Reza is a Consultant Endodontist and the Founder of Care32 Dental Clinic, Ratnagiri. With over thirteenth years of clinical experience, he specializes in advanced root canal treatment and restorative dentistry. He is known for his precision, calm approach, and commitment to preserving natural teeth while ensuring patient comfort.

Dr. Aamir Khan

BDS, MDS | Prosthodontist

Dr. Aamir Khan is a Consultant Prosthodontist specializing in full mouth rehabilitation, crowns and bridges, complete and partial dentures, implant-supported prostheses, and complex smile restoration. He is known for his meticulous treatment planning, precision in restorative dentistry, and commitment to restoring both function and aesthetics. With a patient-centered approach and attention to detail, Dr. Khan helps patients regain confident, healthy smiles through comprehensive prosthodontic care.

Dr. Anas Patel

BDS, MDS (Pedodontist)

Dr. Anas Patel is a dedicated Pedodontist specializing in comprehensive dental care for infants, children, and adolescents. Known for his gentle, compassionate, and child-friendly approach, he creates a comfortable and fun environment that helps young patients feel at ease during treatment. His excellent behavior management skills and caring nature have earned the trust of both children and their parents, making every dental visit a positive experience.

Dr. Anjali Jha

Dr. Anjali Jha is a highly skilled Periodontist with expertise in the diagnosis and treatment of gum diseases, periodontal surgeries, and dental implant support procedures. Known for her clinical precision, gentle approach, and excellent patient management, she is a trusted consulting Periodontist committed to delivering exceptional periodontal care. Her dedication to excellence and patient comfort has earned her the confidence of both patients and fellow dental professionals.

Dr. Sukirti Yaligar

Dr. Sukirti Yaligar is a Consultant Oral & Maxillofacial Surgeon specializing in impacted wisdom tooth removal, facial trauma, complex extractions, and minor oral surgical procedures. Her precise surgical skills, calm demeanor, and patient-focused approach ensure safe, comfortable, and predictable treatment outcomes.

Dr. Arpit Chanchad

Dr.Arpit chanchad is a Consultant Orthodontist with extensive experience in braces and clear aligner therapy. He is known for creating healthy, confident smiles through personalized orthodontic treatment plans and modern techniques. His approachable nature and commitment to excellence have earned him the trust of patients across Mumbai.

Dr. Hemal Reza

Dr.Hemal Reza is an Implantologist and the Co-Founder of Care32 Dental Clinic, Ratnagiri and Rajapur. She specializes in dental implants and smile rehabilitation, with a focus on restoring function and aesthetics. Her attention to detail and patient-first approach make her a trusted clinician for advanced dental treatments.
